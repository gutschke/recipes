Curried Yams

1 medium onion
8 cloves garlic
8 lime leaves
2 tbsp peanut butter
2 tbsp grated ginger
1 tbsp whole cumin
1 tbsp whole coriander
2 tbsp gochugaru
◇
1 tsp coconut oil
1 tbsp bouillon paste
500ml water
1 can coconut milk
2 tbsp sugar
3 large yams
fish or soy sauce
fried shallots

Add curry ingredients to a blender and process into a smooth paste. Add water if needed.

Melt coconut oil in sauce pan and fry curry paste to bloom spices. Then add remaining sauce ingredients and bring to a slow boil. Adjust saltiness with fish or soy sauce to taste.

Cut yams into inch size cubes and cook in curry on low heat until softened.

Optionally, top with fried shallots when serving.
