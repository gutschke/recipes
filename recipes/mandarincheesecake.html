Mandarin Cheesecake

4 egg whites
4 egg yolks
½ cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
¾ cup flour
2 tbsp corn starch
◇
½ cup orange marmalade
◇
4 envelopes gelatin
30oz canned mandarin oranges
1½ lbs cream cheese
1 cup plain yoghurt
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 cup sugar
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1 cup whipping cream

Pre-heat oven to 425°F.

Whip egg whites and half of the sugar until firm.

Whip egg yolks and remaining sugar, then fold in egg whites. Add vanilla extract. Sift flour and corn starch and carefully fold into the dough.

Transfer dough to a spring form and bake for about 10min; check that it has baked completely by probing with a wooden skewer.

Let cake bottom cool in the form. When at room temperature and coat with marmalade.

Drain mandarin oranges saving the juice. Sprinkle gelatine on juice and let sit for about 1 min; then disolve in hot water.

Blend gelatine with cream cheese, yoghurt, half the mandarin oranges, lemon juice and sugar. Whip cream and fold into filling.

Coat cake bottom with filling and refrigerate over night. Decorate with remaining mandarin oranges.
