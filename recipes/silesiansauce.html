Silesian Beer Sauce
Christmas Meal of the Gutschke Family

300g ginger bread
200ml root beer
200ml stout
margarine
flour
◇
1 bunch leeks
⅓ root celeriac
3 carrots
1 parsnip root
salt
cider vinegar
◇
500g salt pork
◇
500g sauerkraut
juniper berries
bay leaves
cloves
allspice
black peppercorns
2 rashers bacon
◇
10 white sausages
1 loaf rye sourdough bread

Soak the gingerbread in root beer and stout. Blend until smooth.

Boil salt pork on low heat for about 1h.

In the meantime, season sauerkraut with juniper berries, bay leaves, cloves, allspice, and peppercorns. Boil until softened, about 1½ hours.

Cut bacon into small bits and fry until crispy. Pour over sauerkraut.

Make a medium-dark roux from margarine and flour and add gingerbread slurry.

In a separate pot, boil chopped leeks, diced celeriac, diced carrots and diced parsnips until softened. About 10-15 minutes. Pour vegetables (including water) into gingerbread sauce and bring to a boil.

Add broth from salt pork and season with salt and vinegar.

Heat up white sausages in water, but don't bring to a boil.

Serve with rye sourdough bread.
