Tiramisu

500ml strong decaf
30ml cognac
15ml whiskey
400g lady fingers
◇
225g mascarpone
225g cream cheese
◇
4 egg whites
1g cream of tartar
◇
8 egg yolks
200g sugar
20ml Amaretto syrup
30ml sweet vermouth
2g vanilla paste
◇
30g cocoa

Lay out half of the lady fingers in a casserole, brush with about half to two-thirds of the coffee, cognac, and whiskey.

Heat the mascarpone and cream cheese in the microwave until starting to soften, about 30s to 50s. Whisk until smooth.

Whip the egg whites and cream of tartar to stiff peaks and fold under the cream cheese.

Whisk the egg yolks with the sugar until creamy, about 1min. Add Amaretto syrup, vermouth and vanilla paste, then transfer to a bain-marie. Keep whisking over a hot waterbath until the zabaglione starts to thicken and has at least tripled in volume.

Gently fold zabaglione into the mascarpone/cream cheese. Work in multiple batches.

Coat the lady fingers with about half of the filling. Dunk remaining lady fingers into coffee and arrange on top of filling. Then pour reminder of filling and make sure the top is level.

Refrigerate for at least 6h or overnight. Dust with cocoa powder before serving
