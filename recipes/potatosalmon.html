Potato Crusted Salmon On A Bed Of Greens

4 medium size potatoes
2 tbsp olive oil
½ cup evaporated milk
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
½ cup chopped shrimp
2 tbsp breadcrumbs
salt
◇
½ side salmon
8 oz baby spinach
8 oz arugula
1 medium bunch tarragon
1 medium bunch dill
½ cup heavy cream
Creole seasoning salt

Boil potatoes in salt water. Combine with olive oil and evaporated milk and mash coarsely. Add feta cheese and shrimp.

Preheat oven to 450°F.

Tear tarragon and dill into 1½" pieces and combine with other greens in a large casserole. Add heavy cream and season with Creole salt to taste.

Remove skin from salmon, wash, and pat dry. Place on top of greens. Then spread an even coating of mashed potatoes on fish, and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.

Place under broiler for about 3-4min. Then continue baking for another 10min.

Serve with homemade pasta.

Serves 4
