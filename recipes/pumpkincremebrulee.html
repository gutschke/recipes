Pumpkin Crème Brûlée

5 egg yolks
350ml heavy cream
70g sugar
12g brown sugar
3g salt
4g cinnamon
1½g nutmgeg
1g ground ginger
½g cloves
3ml vanilla extract
◇
For flan, substitute:
3 egg yolks
1 egg
175ml heavy cream
175ml milk

Preheat oven to 325°F.

Whisk eggs, sugar, and salt. Set aside.

Combine all remaining ingredients and bring to a simmer.

Pour through a fine meshed strainer or chinoise. Then temper the eggs by slowly adding a small amount of the hot custard base. Finally, combine all ingredients and divide among 6 ramekins.

Bake in a water bath to an internal temperature of 170°F or about 35 minutes. Then chill for at least 4 hours.

If making crème brûlée, caramelize white sugar on the top. Skip this step when making flan, but consider serving with caramel sauce.
