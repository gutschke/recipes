House Bread

300g rye flour
180ml water
40g molasses
100g sourdough
◇
225g whole wheat flour
135g water
◇
225g bread flour
110ml water
18g salt
3g caraway
3g coriander
1g fennel
1g fenugreek

Combine ingredients for rye and for whole wheat doughs and let both of them rest overnight.

Freshly grind spices, then combine both prepared doughs and add all the remaining ingredients. Knead until fully incorporated and homogeneous.

Stretch-and-fold over a period of 45 minutes (in about 10-15min increments). Let proof for 3-12 hours depending on ambient temperature.

While preheating oven to 450°F and shape dough. Bench rest for 45min.

Bake for 20min at high humidity (e.g. in a covered Dutch oven), then reduce temperature to 350°F and reduce humidity (e.g. by removing lid). Bake until interior temperature reaches 205°F, about another 40min.
