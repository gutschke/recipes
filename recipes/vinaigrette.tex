\begin{tabular}{ll}
$\frac12$&small onion\\
$1$ cup&vegetable oil\\
$\frac14$ cup&balsamic vinegar\\
$\frac12$ cup&cranberries\\
$1$ tsp&honey mustard\\
$1$ Tbsp&sugar\\
&salt\\
\end{tabular}}

\leavevmode\vbox to .2in{\vss}

Blend the ingredients in a food processor until you get an
emulsion. Store in a cool place until needed.

Serve as dressing on mixed greens. Add apples, walnuts, blue cheese,
etc. to taste.
