Boca Negra Cake

340g 80% cocoa chocolate
230g butter
120ml bourbon
275g sugar
1g salt
1g espresso powder
5 eggs
12g flour

Preheat oven to 400Â°F.

Spray 10" cake pan with oil and add a sheet of baking paper that has been cut to shape.

Break chocolate into small pieces and cut butter into cubes. Bring bourbon, sugar, salt, and espresso powder to a boil. Pour boiling syrup over chocolate and butter. Stir until butter is fully melted, then add eggs, flour.

Pour batter into cake pan and bake in a water bath until a crust forms on the top, or about 30 minutes.

Serve with heavy cream or ice cream. Decorate with fresh fruits
