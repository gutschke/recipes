Ricotta Mousse

400g ricotta
60g sugar
35ml orange blossom water
0.4g cinnamon
◇
3.5g gelatin
◇
240ml heavy cream
40g sugar
◇
1 orange
20ml lemon juice
65g butter
50g sugar
1g salt
40ml Grand Manier
5ml orange bitters
 
 

Process ricotta, sugar, orange blossom water, and cinnamon in a blender until smooth.

Sprinkle gelatin over a small amount of water and melt in the microwave.

Slowly add some of the ricotta to the gelatin to prevent clumping. Then add this mixture into the ricotta and blend until combined.

Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form and fold under ricotta mixture.

Divide into 8 containers and refrigerate until set.

Serve with fresh fruit or warm orange syrup.
