Buckwheat Crêpe

100g buckwheat flour
35g all-purpose flour
½ tsp salt
500g milk
3 eggs
55g melted butter
◇
10 slices cooked ham
250g grated Gruyère cheese
10 eggs
melted butter

Preheat oven to 450°F.

Combine ingredients to make batter and cook 10 crêpe. Let cool on wire rack.

Add a slice of ham, make a “nest” from the cheese and crack an egg into the center. Fold four sides over, brush with melted butter, and bake for 8 to 10 minutes.

Garnish with parsley and urfa biber peppers. Add Cholula sauce to taste.

Lot’s of variations on this classic theme are possible. Consider adding olive tapenade; substitute smoked ham for cooked ham; or even change ingredients to make with smoked salmon and crème frâiche.
