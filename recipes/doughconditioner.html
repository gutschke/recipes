Dough Conditioner

45g potato flour
35g vital wheat gluten
15g corn starch
6g dry yeast
5g diastatic malt
4g lecithin
4g ascorbic acid

Thoroughly combine all ingredients. Use about 1g of conditioner per 35g of flour (or about 3 baker's percent). Use less for white breads and more for heavier doughs (e.g. rye flour, whole wheat, ...).

Possible alternatives: 1) substitute potato starch for corn starch, 2) add about 4g of dried ginger.
