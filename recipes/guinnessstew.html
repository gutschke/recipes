Guinness Stew

4 lbs beef chuck eye roast
2-3 large onions
1 Tbsp tomato paste
2 cloves garlic
¼ cup flour
28 fl oz chicken broth
10 fl oz Guinness
20g brown sugar
1 tsp fresh thyme
1 lbs carrots
1½ lbs potatoes
2 Tbsp parsley
salt
pepper

Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).

Finely chop onions and sautee with some salt and oil in a Dutch oven until deep brown. Add tomato paste and crushed garlic, and stir until brown. Add flour and stir for about two minutes until fully incorporated and lightly browned.

Add chicken broth, ½ cup of Guinness, brown sugar and thyme. Cut meat into 1½" cubes, season with salt and pepper and add to the stew base.

Roast uncovered in the oven for 3½ hours. During the last 1½ hours, add 1" chunks of carrots and potatoes.

Remove from oven and add remaining amount of Guinness, parsley, and salt and pepper to taste.
