Spinach & Wild Green Pie

A savory, herb-forward Mediterranean pie in a flaky olive oil and butter shortcrust.

Ingredients

Hybrid Shortcrust

  • 400g All-purpose flour
  • 6g Fine sea salt
  • 115g Unsalted butter, cold, cubed
  • 80g Extra virgin olive oil
  • 65-75g Ice-cold water

The Filling

  • 900g Fresh spinach
  • 200g Dandelion greens, lower stems trimmed
  • 950g Zucchini (approx. 3 large)
  • 6g Fine sea salt (for sweating)
  • 100g Scallions (spring onions), finely sliced
  • 90g Fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 400g Feta cheese, crumbled
  • 5 Large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 50g Panko breadcrumbs
  • 65g Walnuts, toasted and chopped
  • 1/2 tsp Nutmeg, freshly grated
  • Black pepper, freshly ground
Equipment Note: Prepare a 23x33 cm (9x13 inch) rectangular baking dish.

Preparation & Assembly

1. Vegetable Desiccation Grate the zucchini into a colander, toss with 6g salt, and let sit for 20 minutes. Wilt the spinach and dandelion greens in a steam oven or a large pot. Squeeze the vegetables in a clean kitchen towel with maximum force until remarkably dry. Critical step: Briefly dry-sauté the squeezed greens in a skillet over medium heat for 2-3 minutes to evaporate any remaining surface moisture. Allow to cool.
2. Hand-Worked Dough Whisk flour and salt in a large bowl. Add cold butter cubes. Squeeze the cubes between your thumb and index fingers to flatten them into flakes, then rub lightly until the mixture resembles coarse meal. Stir in the olive oil with a fork. Add ice water 15g at a time, mixing until a shaggy mass forms. Do not knead.
3. Pre-Forming & Resting Divide the dough into a 60% portion (~400g) and a 40% portion (~265g). Press the larger portion evenly into the bottom and 1.5 cm up the sides of the baking dish. Wrap the dish and the remaining dough disc; chill in the refrigerator for at least 45 minutes.
Make-Ahead Protocol: Mix the cooled greens, crumbled feta, scallions, dill, walnuts, nutmeg, and pepper. Store this mixture in the refrigerator. Do not add the eggs until immediately before baking to prevent the salt in the feta from drawing out further moisture. Tip: Taste this mixture for seasoning before adding the raw eggs.
4. Final Assembly Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F). If you skipped the make-ahead step, now combine the cooled greens, feta, scallions, dill, walnuts, nutmeg, and pepper. Stir the beaten eggs into the filling mixture. Sprinkle the panko evenly across the chilled bottom crust to act as a moisture barrier. Spread the filling evenly over the panko.
5. Topping & Baking Roll the remaining dough disc on a lightly floured surface to fit the top of the dish. Place over the filling, crimp the edges to seal against the bottom crust, and score the top into serving-sized diamonds to vent steam.
6. Finishing Bake for 45–55 minutes until the crust is deeply golden brown. Rest the pie for 15 minutes before cutting to allow the internal structure to cool and set.
Yields 1 rectangular pie (23x33 cm) | Oven: 200°C (400°F) | Metric Weights
