Griesbrei (Semolina Pudding)

165g semolina
15g sugar
1g salt
1l milk
2 eggs
cream of tartar

Add semolina, sugar, salt and milk to a sauce pan. Bring to a simmer, and stir regularly with a balloon whisk to avoid clumping.

When pudding starts thickening, take off the heat and stir in two egg yolks. Whisk the egg whites with a pinch of cream of tartar until stiff peaks begin to form. Fold under pudding.

Eat while hot, or refrigerate in a decorative mold and save for later. Serve with cinnamon and sugar, or top with fruit.

Makes 4 servings
