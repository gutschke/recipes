Levantine Siniyah Feast

Baked Lamb in Tahini, Smoked Eggplant Dip, and Jeweled Vermicelli Rice
Master Workflow & Tool Sequencing
  1. Preheat & Roast: Oven to 220°C (425°F). Roast cauliflower (25 mins) and eggplants (35-40 mins) on a sheet pan. Lower oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Meat Prep: Mix panko and milk for panade; let hydrate. Grind spices. Mix Siniyah base in tared bowl, press into pan, bake 15 mins.
  3. Stovetop: Sauté durum vermicelli, ginger, and spices. Add rice, boiling water, chickpeas, and saffron-salt. Simmer.
  4. Blender Tasks: Emulsify Salatet dressing with xanthan gum. Rinse blender, then process the Mutabal ingredients until smooth.
  5. Finish Siniyah: Whisk tahini sauce in a tared bowl; temper with warm water. Drain fat from lamb, pour sauce, finish baking.

Mutabal (Smoked Eggplant Dip)

  • 400g eggplants (approx. 3-4 small/Asian style)
  • 45g premium tahini paste & 30g labneh
  • 15ml fresh lemon juice & 5g fine sea salt
  • 3g garlic (1 clove), microplaned
  • 3g sumac (garnish) & 15ml olive oil
  1. Pierce eggplants; roast at 220°C (425°F) for 35-40 mins until very soft. Scoop flesh.
  2. Place eggplant flesh, tahini, labneh, lemon juice, salt, and garlic into a blender. Process until smooth. Garnish with sumac and oil.

Siniyah (Baked Lamb in Tahini Sauce)

Lamb Base

  • 40g panko breadcrumbs & 60ml whole milk
  • 500g ground lamb
  • 120g onion (1 medium), grated and drained
  • 30g flat-leaf parsley, finely minced
  • 10g sea salt
  • Fresh ground: 3g black pepper, 3g cumin, 3g coriander, 1g cinnamon, 1g cloves, 1g cardamom

Tahini Sauce & Garnish

  • 200g premium tahini paste
  • 70ml fresh lemon juice & zest of 1 lemon
  • 200ml warm water
  • 40g pine nuts, toasted & 2g sumac
  1. Oven at 200°C (400°F). Combine panko and milk; rest 5 mins.
  2. In tared bowl, combine lamb, panade, onion, parsley, salt, and spices. Knead. Press into baking dish (2cm thick). Bake 15 mins.
  3. Wipe bowl. Whisk tahini, lemon juice, and zest. Temper: stream warm water while whisking until velvety.
  4. Drain fat. Pour tahini sauce over lamb. Bake 10-12 mins. Top with pine nuts and sumac.

Spiced Vermicelli Rice with Chickpeas

  • 8g fine sea salt ground with 0.1g saffron threads
  • 45g butter or ghee
  • 50g durum wheat vermicelli, crushed
  • 4g fresh ginger, microplaned
  • 2g cinnamon & 2g cardamom (ground)
  • 1g each: ground cumin, cloves
  • 300g Basmati rice, rinsed well
  • 150g canned chickpeas, rinsed
  • 550ml boiling water
  • 30g fresh dill, chopped & 1g rose petals
  1. Melt butter over medium heat. Sauté vermicelli until golden.
  2. Add ginger, cinnamon, cardamom, cumin, cloves. Toast 30s.
  3. Add rice. Toast 2 mins. Stir in chickpeas, boiling water, and saffron-salt powder.
  4. Cover, simmer on lowest heat 15 mins. Steam off-heat 10 mins. Fluff, fold in dill, garnish with rose petals.

Zahra (Spiced Roasted Cauliflower)

  • 500g cauliflower florets & 30ml olive oil
  • 5g fine sea salt & 0.1g saffron threads (ground together)
  • 5g ground cumin, 4g ground coriander, 1g granulated garlic

Toss florets with oil, saffron-salt, and spices. Roast at 220°C (425°F) for 25 mins.

Salatet Banadora (Tomato & Cucumber Salad)

  • 400g tomatoes & 200g Persian cucumbers, diced
  • 30g scallions, sliced
  • Dressing: 45ml EVOO, 15ml lemon juice, 4g sumac, 3g fine sea salt, 2g sugar, 1g dried mint, 0.1g xanthan gum

Combine dressing ingredients in a blender. Process until fully emulsified and stabilized. Toss with vegetables just before serving.

Quick Turnip & Cured Lemon Snack

  • 250g purple top turnip, sliced thin
  • 15g salt-cured lemon rind, minced
  • 15ml fresh lemon juice & 3g sumac

Toss turnip with cured lemon, juice, and sumac. Stand 10 mins.
